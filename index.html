LillithApp

Your cycle. Your data. Yours to keep.

A private, science-backed menstrual cycle tracker whose data stays encrypted on your own Solid Pod — so you alone own and control it.

In plain words (for anyone, any age): A period-and-body diary that keeps your secrets in a locked box that only you have the key to. No company gets to peek. No one sells it. It's yours.

What problem does it solve?

Most cycle-tracking apps make money from the most private health data there is — your body, your cycle, your fertility. They store it on their servers and can share, sell or leak it.

LillithApp flips that. Your information lives in your own online locker (a Solid "Pod"), scrambled so no one but you can read it — and you decide if anyone else ever sees it.

Kid version: Imagine a diary. Other apps keep your diary in their cupboard and read it. LillithApp gives the diary back to you, locks it, and only you have the key. 🔑

How your data stays yours (Solid)

LillithApp is built on the W3C Solid standard. Here's the whole thing in simple pieces:

1. It reads and writes to your Pod

When you log in (to a Community Solid Server or any Solid server), the app saves your cycle data into your Pod and reads it back — using your own authenticated session. There's no LillithApp database anywhere.

2. Encryption is the heart of it

Everything is written with writePod(encrypted: true). Your Pod stores scrambled data, not readable text. Without your key, it's gibberish — even to whoever runs the server.

Kid version: Your diary is written in a secret code only your key can unlock.

3. You control who sees it (access control & consent)

The Privacy screen uses Solid's access-control rules (WAC/ACP) — grantPermission, readPermission, revokePermission — so you can give one trusted person read-only access after a clear "I consent" step, see exactly who has access, and revoke it whenever you want.

4. Data minimisation

The app keeps only what you actually log. Clear a day, and it's truly gone — empty entries aren't stored.

Try the whole app in one tap

New here? On the Dashboard (or My Profile) tap "Load a month of sample data". It fills your Pod with a realistic month of readings, period starts and symptom logs so every feature works straight away — perfect for a demo.

Is this medical advice?

No. LillithApp gives gentle, science-backed wellness information and estimates from your own data. It is not a medical device and must not be used for contraception or diagnosis. If something worries you, please talk to a doctor, nurse or pharmacist — asking is always okay. 💜

For developers

  • Built with Flutter/Dart on solidpod and solidui.
  • All health data is a single encrypted JSON document in the app's Pod folder.
  • Sharing uses Solid Web Access Control / ACP via grantPermission / readPermission / revokePermission.
  • Login is Solid-OIDC; this page's client-profile.jsonld lists the app's redirect URIs.
